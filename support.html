Getting Started

Your 12-week program is designed specifically for women 45+ who want gentle, effective workouts.

  • 7 workouts per week — each day has a different focus (yoga, walking, cardio, etc.)
  • Built-in rest days — your body needs time to recover
  • 10-25 minutes each — short enough to fit any schedule
  • Progressive difficulty — starts easy and gradually builds

Simply open your dashboard each day, tap Start Workout, and follow along!

Your dashboard has 3 main tabs at the bottom:

  • My Plan — Your daily workout, weekly schedule, and progress stats
  • Workouts — Browse all 21+ workouts in the library by category
  • Profile — Your goals, settings, and account info

Tap the week strip at the top to see workouts for different days. Each day is tailored for you!

No equipment needed! All workouts use just your body weight.

For chair workouts, you'll need a sturdy chair — any dining or kitchen chair works perfectly.

Tip: A yoga mat is nice to have for floor exercises but isn't required. A carpet or thick towel works fine too.

You can adjust your difficulty level anytime:

  • Go to Profile tab
  • Tap Update Goals
  • Change your Difficulty Level to Easy, Beginner, or Moderate

Remember: listen to your body. It's always okay to pause, take breaks, or skip an exercise. The best workout is the one you actually do!

Account & Profile

To log in:

  • Go to lazyweightloss.com
  • Click the Log In button in the top right
  • Enter your email and password
  • You'll be redirected to your dashboard
Forgot your password? We're working on adding password reset. For now, please contact us and we'll help you regain access.

To update your profile:

  • Go to the Profile tab
  • Tap Personal Info
  • Update your display name, age, or height
  • Tap Save Changes

Your display name will appear in the greeting at the top of your dashboard.

You can update your goals anytime:

  • Go to the Profile tab
  • Tap Update Goals
  • Adjust your current weight, goal weight, daily workout time, and more
  • Tap Save Changes

Your profile stats will update immediately to reflect the changes.

Your complete workout history is available in the Profile tab:

  • Tap Progress History
  • See your total workouts, minutes, calories, and streak
  • Browse your recent activity with dates and details

Every completed workout is automatically logged — just finish the workout and tap Done to save it.

Troubleshooting

If an exercise video isn't loading, try these steps:

  • Check your internet connection — videos need a stable connection to stream
  • Refresh the page — sometimes a quick reload fixes loading issues
  • Try a different browser — Chrome and Safari work best
  • Disable ad blockers — they can sometimes block video embeds

Don't worry — if a video can't load, the workout still works! You'll see an animated emoji guide showing each exercise.

Your progress and settings are saved in your browser. If things aren't saving:

  • Make sure you complete workouts — tap the "Done" button at the end to log it
  • Don't use private/incognito mode — data isn't saved between sessions in private browsing
  • Use the same browser — progress is stored per browser, so switching between Chrome and Safari means separate data
  • Don't clear browser data — clearing cookies/storage will erase your saved progress
Tip: Your login session is separate from workout data. Even if you log in on a new device, workout history may not transfer since it's stored locally.

If the dashboard or workout page isn't loading:

  • Hard refresh — Press Ctrl+Shift+R (or Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)
  • Check if you're logged in — You may need to sign in again
  • Try a different device — Open lazyweightloss.com on your phone or tablet
  • Wait a moment — Occasionally there may be brief downtime during updates

If the problem persists, please contact us with a screenshot and we'll help right away.
